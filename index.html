Free Music Playlist (Jamendo App Contest)

The Free Music PlayList is a site that integrates Jamendo, SoundCloud, FMA and Last.fm to create playlists, getting song and artist information and recommendations. Share your playlist with your friends by sending them the link. Save your playlist by bookmarking it. Search for music by artist name, tagname... Discover the most popular songs from different sites. Add music by introducing the URL of a site from those cloud services. Get songs recommended based on a Last.fm username.
